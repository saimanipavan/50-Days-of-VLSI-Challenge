# Week 1 – Combinational Logic Design

## Overview

Week 1 of the **50 Days VLSI Challenge** focuses on building a strong foundation in **Combinational Logic Design** using **Verilog HDL** and **Gate-Level Modeling**. Throughout the week, fundamental digital circuits were designed, verified through simulation, and documented with RTL schematics and waveforms.

The week concludes with a **4-Bit Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU)** that integrates several of the previously designed modules into a hierarchical digital system.
